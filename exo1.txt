Exercise: Build Your First HTML Webpage
🎯 Goal

Create a simple webpage about yourself and your favorite hobby.

Your webpage must include:

✅ A main heading with your name.

✅ A paragraph introducing yourself.

✅ A second heading called "My Favorite Hobby".

✅ A paragraph explaining why you like this hobby.

✅ One image.

✅ One link to your favorite website or YouTube video.

✅ One audio OR one video.

✅ An unordered list of your favorite foods.

✅ An ordered list of three things you want to learn.

Example Structure
My Name

Hello! My name is ...

My Favorite Hobby

I enjoy ...

(Image)

(Link)

(Video)

Favorite Foods
• Pizza
• Burger
• Ice Cream

Things I Want to Learn
